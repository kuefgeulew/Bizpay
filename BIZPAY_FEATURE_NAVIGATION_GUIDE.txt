Navigate all 70+ screens without confusion

5️⃣ FEATURE PURPOSE GUIDE
🏦 CORE BANKING OPERATIONS
MFS Transfer
Enables mobile financial service payments through Bangladesh's top providers (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, SureCash, Upay) for retail and vendor payments.

Own Account Transfer
Internal fund movement between business accounts for liquidity management, allocation, and fund positioning. Supports single and bulk transfers.

Third Party Transfer
External beneficiary payments with mandatory approval workflows, beneficiary governance, and fraud prevention controls.

Direct Debit
Automated recurring payment mandate setup for rent, utilities, subscriptions, insurance premiums with maker-checker control and mandate management.

Bill Payment
Utility bill payment workflows for electricity (DESCO, DPDC), gas (Titas, Jalalabad), WASA, and government payments with category-based organization.

✅ CONTROL & GOVERNANCE
Approval Queue
Central hub for reviewing and approving pending transactions. Demonstrates dual-control maker-checker separation with role-based approval authority.

Transaction Limits
Role-based spending authority configuration. Defines per-user, per-role, and per-transaction-type limits to prevent unauthorized high-value payments.

Escalation Rules
Automatic transaction routing to higher authority when limits are exceeded. Configurable escalation chains (Maker → Checker → Approver → Admin).

Beneficiary Governance
Complete beneficiary lifecycle management with approval workflows, cooling periods, risk scoring, and fraud prevention controls.

Activity Log
Immutable audit trail of all user actions, approvals, and system events. Provides forensic-grade logging for compliance, security analysis, and dispute resolution.

💡 INTELLIGENCE & INSIGHTS
Smart Alerts (Behavioral Cash Insights)
CFO-grade behavioral intelligence providing runway warnings, unusual pattern detection, seasonal signals, and cash behavior nudges. Read-only, non-restrictive intelligence.

Cash Intelligence
Cash runway projection, minimum safe balance calculation, seasonality analysis, and forecasting for strategic cash management and liquidity planning.

Risk Dashboard
Real-time fraud monitoring, anomaly detection, beneficiary risk scoring, suspicious activity flagging, and security event tracking.

Admin Control Tower
Enterprise oversight dashboard showing approval health metrics, user risk scores, system integrity checks, workflow bottlenecks, and control effectiveness.

Timeline View
Visual calendar of all scheduled payments, EMI obligations, bill due dates, and upcoming cash commitments for proactive cash flow planning.

📊 RECEIVABLES & COLLECTIONS
Virtual Account Management (VAM)
Distributor receivables tracking using virtual account numbers for automatic payment tagging, client-wise collection monitoring, and receivables reconciliation.

Collections Hub
Central navigation for all collection-related features: VAM, direct debit collection mandates, and collection matching/reconciliation workflows.

Invoice Hub
GST invoice tracking, outstanding receivables aging, invoice-wise payment matching, and overdue customer analysis with drill-down capabilities.

Receivables Intelligence (Placeholder)
AR Dashboard with overdue customer tracking, auto-nudge triggers, receivable health scoring, and collection optimization recommendations.

🔄 RECONCILIATION & CASH OPERATIONS
Reconciliation Dashboard
Automatic transaction matching engine with AI-based pattern recognition, exception management, manual matching for edge cases, and rule configuration.

Exception Management
Handles unmatched transactions, partial matches, amount discrepancies, and suspicious pattern detection during reconciliation processes.

Auto-Reconciliation Rules
Configures intelligent matching logic based on amount, date, reference patterns, client history, and confidence thresholds for automated reconciliation.

Sweep & Park (Placeholder)
Auto-sweep rules for idle balance optimization, reverse sweep triggers, liquidity pooling across accounts, and automated fund positioning.

Cash Locks (Placeholder)
No-debit window configuration for payroll protection, daily outflow caps, emergency cash preservation, and calendar-based withdrawal restrictions.

💰 STRATEGIC CASH FEATURES
Account Control Tower
Consolidated multi-account view across all business banking relationships to visualize liquidity fragmentation, optimize fund placement, and manage cash concentration.

Cash Buckets (Mental Accounting)
Purpose-based cash allocation (Payroll, Tax, Vendor, Free Cash) as visual-only non-restrictive tool for disciplined cash management without enforcement.

Tax Vault
GST/TDS accumulation tracker with one-tap statutory payment workflow. Separates tax obligations from operating cash for clarity and compliance.

Benefits & Incentives
Non-interest CASA rewards program. Privilege tiers, fee waivers, free payroll runs earned through balance discipline. Anchor Feature 12 of BizPay Cash OS.

💳 PAYMENTS INTELLIGENCE (Placeholders)
Collect Money (Placeholder)
Bangla QR code generation, payment links, virtual account creation, and T+0 settlement for inbound collections with multi-channel payment acceptance.

Outflow Controls (Placeholder)
Scheduled payment delays, maker-checker time windows, intentional friction for large outflows, and configurable hold periods for fraud prevention.

Payables Intelligence (Placeholder)
Optimal pay date calculator balancing early payment discounts vs. float benefit vs. late payment penalties for working capital optimization.

Credit Backstop (Placeholder)
OD/CC auto-utilization as temporary fallback when payment would breach safe cash floor. Protects cash discipline without blocking critical payments (NOT loan initiation).

🛠️ SERVICES & ADMINISTRATION
User Management
User creation, role assignment, permission matrix configuration, and user lifecycle management for enterprise access control and segregation of duties.

Positive Pay
Cheque clearance safety mechanism requiring pre-authorization of cheque details (amount, date, payee) to prevent fraud and unauthorized cheque clearing.

Chequebook Inventory
Cheque leaf tracking, serial number management, usage monitoring, and inventory alerts for physical payment instrument control and audit compliance.

Service Requests
Central menu for all non-transaction banking services: chequebook requests, positive pay setup, token management, and statement requests.

Notification Center
Real-time feed of approval requests, system events, limit breaches, security alerts, and workflow notifications with priority categorization and filtering.
